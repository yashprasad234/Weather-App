About This App

The Weather App is a simple web application that allows you to check the weather for any city. It retrieves real-time weather data using the OpenWeatherMap API, providing you with accurate and up-to-date information.

With the Weather App, you can easily find out the current weather conditions, including the temperature and a brief description of the weather, for any city around the world. Simply enter the name of the city in the search bar, click the "Search" button, and the app will display the weather information below.

Stay informed about the weather in your area or plan your trips by checking the weather conditions in your destination city with the Weather App. Enjoy the convenience of accessing weather information at your fingertips!
